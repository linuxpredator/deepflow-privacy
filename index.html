Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how DeepFlow ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application DeepFlow - Focus Timer (the "App").

Information We Collect

DeepFlow is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Usage Data (Stored Locally): The App stores your timer sessions, statistics, streaks, and preferences locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.
  • No Account Required: DeepFlow does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

Advertising

The free version of DeepFlow displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google AdMob may collect certain information to serve personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • Ad interaction data

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

Premium Users: If you upgrade to DeepFlow Premium, all advertisements are removed and no advertising-related data collection occurs.

Data Storage

All your app data, including:

  • Timer settings and preferences
  • Session history and statistics
  • Streak information
  • Theme preferences

is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data, and it is not backed up to any cloud service unless you use your device's built-in backup features.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties, except as described in the Advertising section above regarding Google AdMob for free users.

Children's Privacy

DeepFlow is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over your data. You can delete all app data by:

  • Uninstalling the app
  • Clearing the app's data through your device settings
  • Using the reset option within the app (if available)

Security

Your data is stored securely on your device using standard mobile storage mechanisms. Since we do not transmit or store your data on external servers, the security of your data depends on your device's security measures.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (stored locally on your device)
  • Delete your data (by uninstalling the app or clearing app data)
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
